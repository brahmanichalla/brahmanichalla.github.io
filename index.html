Research

AlGaN-Channel HEMTs for Ultra-Wide Bandgap Power Electronics

Developing AlGaN-channel HEMTs (<50% Al) via MOCVD for high-voltage, high-frequency power switching. Focus areas include epitaxial growth optimization, device architecture design, and breakdown voltage characterization.

MOCVDAlGaN/GaNUWBGPower Electronics

Selective Regrown n-AlGaN Ohmic Contacts

Designed reverse-graded n-AlGaN ohmic contacts via selective regrowth, achieving low contact resistance critical for high-performance HEMT operation. Includes process development and TLM-based electrical characterization.

TCAD Simulation & Device Characterization

Sentaurus TCAD simulations to analyze breakdown voltage and electric field distribution in AlGaN-channel devices, complemented by Hall effect, HRXRD, AFM, and I-V measurements.
